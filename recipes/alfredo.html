Alfredo Sauce

Fettuccine Alfredo is a great alternative to tomato pasta sauce. It is creamy and cheesy and generally jsut magnificent. This dish is good for a cold day becuase it will warm you up from the inside. You can make additions to your dish such as broccoli or chicken. This dish is super easy to make and everyone will love it.

Ingredientd

  • 1 block of cream cheese
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1+ cup of milk
  • 1 cup of Parmesean cheese

Steps

  1. First you want to melt the butter and cream cheese together on low heat.
  2. Next you will add athe milk and Parmesean to your mixture.
  3. If you would like your sauce garlicky, you can always add some garlic powder at the end.

There you have it you just need to boil some pasta in water and top with the sauce. Now you are ready for dinner!
